University Polytechnic

The present University Polytechnic was established on the initiative of Dr. K. L. Shrimali, then Rural development and Higher education minister. This department, namely ‘Rural Institute’ ran two courses (i). ‘Rural services’ and (ii). ‘Civil and Rural Engineering’ with an intake of 30 students in each course and was governed by the ministry of education.
